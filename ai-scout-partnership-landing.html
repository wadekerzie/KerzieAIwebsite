Compensation Structure

Performance-Based Income Model

Your income is tied directly to the recurring revenue generated by the automation systems Kerzie AI deploys. No salary caps. Income scales with the opportunities you identify and qualify.

  • Partners earn up to 50% of setup and recurring revenue
  • Income is monthly and uncapped
  • This model requires consistent effort and relationship building

A single automation sold at $500/month with a $500 setup fee at 50% commission = $250 upfront + $250/month recurring. That represents $3,250 in your first year from one client, with ongoing monthly payments.
