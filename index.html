FAQ

Clear answers, no hype.

Do we need a big tech stack to work with you?
No. We typically integrate with the tools you already use (email, spreadsheets, CRMs, scheduling, accounting). If you need a better foundation, we’ll recommend it with ROI in mind.
Who will we work with—an AI bot or a real person?
Real people, white-glove. A scout interfaces with your team to understand your workflow and define the best first automation. Then a Deployment Officer sits down with you to securely connect your systems, request the right access, and deploy everything end-to-end.
Will AI replace our team?
Our goal is to remove repetitive work and reduce errors—so your people can focus on customers, quality, and growth. We design automations with human approvals and clear ownership.
How do you handle security and privacy?
Least-privilege access, audit trails, and clear data boundaries. We avoid over-collection, and we’ll tailor controls to your compliance needs.
What does the first month look like?
We scout your workflows, choose a high-impact first automation, then your Deployment Officer helps connect the right tools and deploy it. After the first win, we build a roadmap and expand in waves. You’ll see tangible progress quickly.
