Frequently asked questions

Common questions about NemoClaw

What is NemoClaw?

NemoClaw is NVIDIA's enterprise AI agent platform — a controlled version of OpenClaw with enterprise guardrails (what NVIDIA calls "open shell"). It lets you run fleets of AI agents on your own infrastructure. Agents that understand your organization, execute complete workflows, and operate 24/7 within the boundaries you set.

What's the difference between a chatbot and an AI agent?

A chatbot answers questions. An AI agent does the work. NemoClaw agents have full context of your organization — your systems, your processes, your data — and can autonomously execute complete workflows like dispatch, scheduling, documentation, customer service, and reporting. They're digital employees, not chat widgets.

How long does the install take?

One week. Day 1 is agent strategy mapping and platform deployment. Days 2–4 cover security, SSO, messaging, and API integrations. Day 5 delivers agent provisioning training, the playbook, and a First 5 Agents workshop where your team stands up production agents hands-on.

What industries does this work for?

Any company with operational complexity. We have deep experience in construction and logistics, but NemoClaw deploys for any industry — manufacturing, professional services, healthcare operations, property management, and more. If you have admin bottlenecks or coordination overhead, NemoClaw can help.

Do I need technical staff to use it?

No. We handle the technical deployment. After install, your team provisions agents using a playbook designed for operations leaders, not engineers. We also offer 30/60/90-day support tiers with hands-on training if you want guided assistance after launch.
