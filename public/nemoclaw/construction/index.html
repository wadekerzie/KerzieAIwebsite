What NemoClaw does in your operation

5 AI agent roles. Built for construction.

01

Dispatch & Scheduling Agent

Monitors inbound requests, extracts job details, proposes crew assignments by skill/availability/location, updates your dispatch system, and drafts customer confirmations. Flags conflicts for human approval.

  • Triages every inbound request — phone, email, text, portal
  • Matches jobs to crews by skill, cert, proximity
  • Auto-updates your field service software
  • Sends confirmations and appointment reminders
02

Work Order & Documentation Agent

Converts field notes, voice memos, and photos into structured work orders. Populates materials, labor, tasks, and follow-ups. Generates compliance docs. Pushes clean records into your systems.

  • Raw field data → structured work orders
  • Auto-fills materials, hours, task completions
  • Generates inspection/compliance documentation
  • Flags missing info, requests from field staff automatically
03

Estimating & Proposal Assistant

Reads plans, specs, and RFQs. Pulls from past jobs and pricing templates. Drafts proposals, SOWs, and change orders. Prepares client-ready document packages. Learns from every approved estimate.

  • Ingests blueprints, specs, and RFQs
  • Cross-references historical job data and pricing
  • Drafts proposals, SOWs, change orders
  • Improves accuracy with every approved estimate
04

Invoicing & Close-Out Agent

Watches for job completions. Pulls time, materials, and scope data to draft invoices. Checks for missing entries. Assembles close-out packages — reports, photos, docs — and delivers to client.

  • Monitors job completion across systems
  • Auto-drafts invoices from time/materials data
  • Catches missing entries before finalizing
  • Assembles and delivers close-out packages
05

Field Knowledge Assistant

On mobile for field staff. Answers questions from company manuals, past jobs, and SOPs. Suggests troubleshooting steps. Links to relevant docs and specs. Captures tribal knowledge over time.

  • Entire knowledge base in a tech's pocket
  • Answers from manuals, SOPs, job history
  • Suggests troubleshooting steps per equipment
  • New hires ramp in days, not months
