# NemoClaw Post-Install Support Tiers — Strategy Doc

**Status:** Draft — validate in first 2-3 install calls before adding to website.

---

## Baseline (included with every $22,500 install)
- Provisioning playbook delivered on Day 5
- 1 reference agent configured and live
- 2-hour Command Center Orientation session on Day 5

---

## Tier 1: Launch Support — 30 days / $3,500/mo

**For teams that want guided liftoff.**

- Weekly 1-hour strategy call (4 total)
- Agent provisioning coaching: how to define roles, write task briefs, set guardrails
- Async Slack/Teams support channel (next-business-day response)
- "First 5 Agents" workshop — hands-on session to stand up first 5 production agents together
- Monthly health check: are agents being used? What's stuck?

**Why it works:** Covers the critical 30-day adoption window where habits form. Most teams will know within 30 days whether they're getting value.

---

## Tier 2: Scale Support — 60 days / $3,000/mo

**For teams ready to expand across departments.**

- Everything in Tier 1, plus:
- Bi-weekly calls (8 total across 60 days)
- Cross-department expansion planning (e.g., started with dispatch → now build agents for estimating and invoicing)
- Agent performance review: which agents are working, which need tuning
- 1 custom agent buildout per month (we configure it, they learn from watching)

**Price logic:** Longer commitment = slight discount. Rewards commitment, not urgency.

---

## Tier 3: Enterprise Partnership — 90 days / $2,500/mo

**For companies that want a fractional AI operations advisor.**

- Everything in Tiers 1 & 2, plus:
- Monthly on-site or deep-dive virtual session (half-day)
- Quarterly roadmap: what's coming in NemoClaw, what new agents to plan for
- Priority support (same-day response)
- Executive briefing: 30-minute monthly update for leadership on ROI, adoption metrics, and what's working

**Price logic:** Best per-month rate, longest lock-in. 90 days at $2,500/mo = $7,500 total. Combined with $22,500 install = $30K engagement per client.

## Strategic Notes

1. **Training is the wedge, not the product.** We're selling confidence. The buyer's fear: "I just spent $22,500 and my team won't use it." Support tiers remove that fear.

2. **30 days should be the default recommendation.** On the install call: "The platform will be live on Friday. Most teams benefit from 30 days of guided support to get their first agents productive — want me to include that?"

3. **Don't discount the install to fund training.** Keep $22,500 firm. Support tiers are additive revenue.

4. **90-day tier is the retention play.** 3 months of recurring revenue + 3 months to create a case study and referral source.

5. **"Just a chatbot" objection dies in the First 5 Agents workshop.** When they see an agent autonomously triage 50 dispatch requests, update their FSM, and send status texts — while they watch — the misconception is gone.

6. **Don't add to website yet.** Validate the framing in the first 2-3 calls. Then add a "Post-Install Support" section to both landing pages.
