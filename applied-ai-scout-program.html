Applied AI Scout Agreement

This Applied AI Scout Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between Kerzie Consulting LLC, a Texas limited liability company, doing business as Kerzie AI Solutions, with its principal place of business at 6005 Autumn Way, McKinney, TX 75072 ("Company"), and the individual or entity who submits the onboarding form and electronically signs this Agreement ("Scout").

Purpose

Scout will identify and introduce qualified prospective customers to the Company's applied AI and automation services. The Company retains sole discretion to accept or reject any prospect. Scout is eligible for compensation only as expressly defined in this Agreement.

Independent Contractor Relationship

Scout is an independent contractor and not an employee, partner, agent, or joint venturer of the Company. Scout has no authority to bind the Company.

Scout is solely responsible for all federal, state, and local taxes, insurance, licenses, permits, and business expenses related to their activities.

Scope of Work

Scout Will:

  • Identify qualified business prospects aligned with the Company's target customer profile
  • Submit prospects into the Company's CRM with complete and accurate information
  • Use only approved messaging and materials
  • Conduct outreach professionally, ethically, and lawfully

Scout Will Not:

  • Represent themselves as an employee or authorized representative
  • Quote pricing, guarantee outcomes, or negotiate contracts
  • Collect sensitive personal information
  • Use deceptive or unlawful outreach practices

Lead Qualification and Attribution

A lead is considered qualified only if:

  • It represents a legitimate business with expressed interest
  • Is not already active in Company systems
  • Is first submitted by Scout with required CRM fields completed

The Company's determination regarding lead qualification and attribution is final.

Compensation

Setup Fee Commission

Scout earns 20% of the setup fee, currently $500, equal to $100 per customer. The setup fee commission is earned only after the customer is contracted, deployed, billed, and paid.

Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) Commission

Scout earns commission on collected monthly recurring revenue based on the number of active, paying customers attributed to the Scout at the time of payout:

Customers 1–2 20% of collected MRR
Customers 3–9 35% of collected MRR
Customers 10+ 50% of collected MRR

Commission tiers adjust if customers cancel or stop paying.

Payout Schedule

  • Payouts are issued monthly only, on the 15th of each month
  • Commissions are paid for customers who were billed and paid in the prior calendar month
  • Payments are issued via ACH or an approved electronic payment method

Refunds and Clawbacks

No commission is earned on unpaid, refunded, or charged-back invoices. The Company may offset future commissions to recover previously paid amounts related to refunded revenue.

Confidentiality

Scout agrees to protect all non-public Company information, including customer data, pricing, internal materials, systems, and processes. Confidentiality obligations survive termination.

Intellectual Property

All Company branding, materials, training content, and systems remain the exclusive property of the Company. Scout receives a limited, revocable license to use these materials only while active and compliant.

Termination

The Company may terminate Scout participation immediately for violations of this Agreement or the Code of Conduct. Upon termination, Scout access to Company systems and materials ends immediately.

Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Texas.

Applied AI Scout Code of Conduct

Purpose

This Code of Conduct defines expectations for how Applied AI Scouts represent Kerzie AI Solutions and interact with prospects and customers.

Professional Conduct

Scouts must communicate respectfully, professionally, and honestly at all times.

Brand Representation

Scouts must represent themselves as Applied AI Scouts, not employees, and must use only approved messaging and materials. Guarantees, exaggerated claims, or unapproved pricing statements are prohibited.

Ethical Outreach

Scouts may not engage in spam, unlawful mass messaging, deceptive claims, impersonation, or high-pressure tactics.

Data Privacy

Scouts must protect prospect data, use Company systems exclusively for lead submission, and never sell, share, or reuse prospect data outside the program.

Violations

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in immediate removal from the program and forfeiture of unpaid commissions related to the violation.
