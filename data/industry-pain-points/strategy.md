# Kerzie.ai — Industry Pain Points Sales Strategy

**Target Market:** SMBs in McKinney, TX & the DFW Metroplex (Applicable Nationwide)
**Objective:** Transition sales positioning from educational ("you need more leads") to structurally indisputable ("you are X, therefore you have Y problem, and here is our solution").

---

## Executive Summary

When selling AI solutions to SMBs, the greatest point of friction is the "educational loop." Business owners often deny they have a problem with lead management because acknowledging it feels like an admission of operational failure. To bypass this resistance, Kerzie.ai must pivot from selling lead capture to solving structurally indisputable bottlenecks.

By framing the problem as a natural byproduct of their specific business model—rather than a failure of management—you validate their experience and instantly build credibility.

This report identifies five SMB industries highly concentrated in the McKinney/North DFW corridor. For each, we have isolated specific, structurally baked-in operational breakdowns that occur daily, cost real revenue, and can be solved immediately with Kerzie.ai's automation and conversational AI capabilities.

---

## The Sales Framework

**Do not say:** "We can help you capture more leads and stop missing calls." (Implies they are doing a bad job).

**Instead say:** "Because you are in [Industry], the structure of your business dictates that [Specific Problem] is happening right now, costing you [X amount]. You can't fix it by hiring more people. You fix it with this specific AI workflow." (Validates their reality and positions AI as the only logical solution).

## Industries

### 1. Roofing Contractors (Storm Chasers & Residential)
- **Problem:** The 48-Hour Post-Storm Lead Surge
- **Stats:** 500% lead spike after hail, 80% of callers don't leave voicemail, 21x more likely to convert if contacted within 5 minutes
- **Solution:** AI voice receptionist + automated SMS follow-up "Surge Mode"
- **Landing Page:** /roofing

### 2. Dental Practices
- **Problem:** The Unscheduled Treatment Plan Graveyard
- **Stats:** $500K–$1M in unscheduled treatment plans per practice
- **Solution:** Backend data-mining + conversational AI outreach for reactivation
- **Landing Page:** /dental

### 3. Property Management Companies
- **Problem:** The "Handoff Bottleneck" in Maintenance Requests
- **Stats:** 4+ manual handoffs per request, 10%+ premium on delayed after-hours repairs
- **Solution:** Intelligent workflow automation as central router
- **Landing Page:** /property-management

### 4. Independent Auto Repair & Body Shops
- **Problem:** The Estimate Approval Delay
- **Stats:** 15–20% daily productivity lost waiting for approvals
- **Solution:** Automated interactive SMS approval system
- **Landing Page:** /auto-repair

### 5. Med Spas and Aesthetic Clinics
- **Problem:** The Post-Consultation Follow-Up Gap
- **Stats:** 20–30% no-show rate, 5–7 follow-up touchpoints needed to close
- **Solution:** Intelligent lead nurturing and retention engine
- **Landing Page:** /med-spa
